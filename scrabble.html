Scrabble Birthday Cake

Cake for my awesome mom! HUGE thank you to Kello on Cake Central who provided soooo much help and inspiration! Cake is German chocolate, filled with chocolate mousse, covered in dark chocolate ganache, and then fondant. Score sheet, letters and letter trays are a fondant/gumpaste mixture. Images over board and score sheet are edible. Scrabble words on the front are a fondant/gumpaste mixture with gold luster dust.
