Peacock Birthday Cake

This cake was made for a 21st birthday party. The body of the peacock was formed out of rice crispy treats and then covered with fondant. The designs on the sides were made with the help of an extruder and were fondant as well. All other accents were fondant, except for the dragees on the tail.
